Hello, my name is Nicholas Malacarne, and I am a developer. My work is primarily web based, and I love learning and using both front-end and back-end technologies. I am currently employed at CraterDust, LLC, and I am loving every minute of it.

I graduated from Gwinnett Tech with an Associate of Applied Science Degree - Computer Programming. The curriculum comprised mainly of Jave/JavaEE, but I was also subjected to other languages such as PHP, Python, JavaScript, and C++. I fell in love with web development at first sight, and I was especially intrigued by technologies that dealt with the communication between client and server such as AJAX.

Since the day I graduated, I have worked in a few professional development environments. My first job was maintaining a legacy PHP web application. I learned many things about real world development during this time. and I mainly worked on tickets, helped develop an web-based electronic signature system using C#, and helped move the project from SVN to Git.

I was later offered a position at CraterDust, LLC which was extremely exciting. The first thing I worked on was a Facebook application for a campaign. The client provided us with Woobox to work with, which did not allow us complete freedom. I ended up working around some of Woobox's limitations by hosting part of the application on Heroku using NodeJS and ExpressJS. Since then, I have built an in-house job tracking web application with Laravel, and I am currently working on an ECommerce migraton from Drupal to Shopify.

In my spare time, I like to learn new languages, libraries, and technologies. I also enjoy writing scripts for the computer games that I play. My social life is comprised of hanging out with my super awesome partner, talking nerdy with my father, and the occasional table-top gaming session.

I built this site so that I could document the many programming adventures which I undertake, and I would also like to write about what I learn along the way.
